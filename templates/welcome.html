Welcome!

What is FULU Finance?

FULU Finance is a stock trading platform that allows you to buy and sell stocks

What can I trade?

We allow you to trade any stock that is listed on the NYSE (New York Stock Exchange)

How does it work?

Quote

Here is truly where the fun begins.

Input your stock symbol into the given field to receive back the current price of a single share in US Dollars

Example:

Let's say you quote Apple (AAPL) shares. You can expect an output like:
A share of Apple Inc. Common Stock (AAPL) costs $221.51

Buy

Now let's make some money.

Input a stock symbol and the amount of shares you would like to purchase

Example:

Let's say you want to buy 5 shares of Apple (AAPL). Make sure to input "AAPL" into stock symbol and "5" into shares. After that, you should be able to see it listed on your portfolio. (Well, provided you have enough money to buy it of course :P)

Sell

Well I hope you are atleast making a profit

Input a stock symbol and the amount of shares you would like to sell

Example:

Let's say you want to sell 5 shares of Apple (AAPL). Make sure to input "AAPL" into stock symbol and "5" into shares. After that, you should be able to see your holdings decrease and your cash balance increase.
